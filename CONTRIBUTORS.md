## 🤝 How to Contribute

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Clone** your fork locally
3. **Create** a feature branch
4. **Make** your changes
5. **Test** thoroughly
6. **Submit** a pull request

See our [Contributing Guide](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for detailed instructions!
